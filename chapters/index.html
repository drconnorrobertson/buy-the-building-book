10 Chapters to Building Ownership

From understanding the opportunity to closing on your first building, every step is covered.

  1. The Rent Trap: Why Business Owners Stay Stuck

    Understand the psychology and misinformation that keeps 73% of business owners renting when they could be building equity. Learn to recognize the hidden costs of leasing and the true opportunity cost of every rent check.

  2. The Building Ownership Mindset

    Shift your thinking from tenant to owner. This chapter covers the mental models of business owners who build generational wealth, and how to evaluate every business decision through the lens of real estate opportunity.

  3. Finding Your First Commercial Building

    How to identify, evaluate, and select the right commercial property for your business. Cover market analysis, property types, location strategy, and due diligence checklists that protect you from costly mistakes.

  4. Financing Strategies: SBA 504, Conventional, and Creative

    Master the financing landscape from SBA 504 loans (10% down) to conventional commercial mortgages, seller financing, and creative structures. Learn which option fits your situation and how to present yourself as the ideal borrower.

  5. The Sale-Leaseback: Your Most Powerful Tool

    The sale-leaseback structure lets you own the building in a separate entity and lease it back to your operating company. This chapter details the setup, legal structure, tax implications, and ongoing management of this wealth-building engine.

  6. Tax Strategies for Building Owners

    Depreciation, cost segregation, bonus depreciation, 1031 exchanges, Opportunity Zones, and interest deductions. This chapter is your comprehensive guide to reducing your tax burden by 30-50% through strategic property ownership.

  7. Self-Directed IRA and Solo 401(k) Purchases

    Use your retirement funds to purchase commercial real estate tax-free. Learn the rules, the restrictions, the custodians, and the step-by-step process for buying your building inside a self-directed retirement account.

  8. Building Equity and Forcing Appreciation

    Commercial property values are driven by income, not just comparables. Learn how to increase your building's value through strategic improvements, lease structuring, and NOI optimization that can double your equity in 5 years.

  9. Managing Your Building as a Business Asset

    Property management, maintenance planning, insurance, and tenant relations (even when you are the tenant). Structure your ownership for maximum efficiency and minimum headache while protecting your investment.

  10. The Exit: Selling Your Business, Keeping Your Building

    The ultimate wealth strategy: sell your operating business for a premium, retain the real estate, and lease it back to the new owner. This chapter covers deal structuring, valuation, and creating a passive income stream for life.
