About the Author

Dr. Connor Robertson has spent his career at the intersection of tax strategy and real estate investment. He advises business owners across the country on how to leverage commercial property ownership to dramatically reduce their tax burden while building long-term wealth.

His approach is built on a simple observation: the wealthiest business owners do not just run great companies. They own the buildings those companies operate in. By combining advanced tax planning (cost segregation, bonus depreciation, 1031 exchanges) with practical acquisition strategies (SBA 504 loans, sale-leasebacks, self-directed retirement accounts), he helps clients keep millions more of what they earn.

"Buy The Building, Keep The Profits" distills years of client work into a clear, actionable playbook that any business owner can follow. No jargon. No fluff. Just the strategies that work.
